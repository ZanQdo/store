Natural Lighting 3.0 for Cycles & EEVEE

Wouldn't it be lovely if all the shadows and details of a forest, a park or a city would come packed inside a single light source?

Bring the beautiful complexity of real world lighting into your Blender scenes with Natural Lighting for Cycles and EEVEE!


Regular CGI lights are plain, boring and static, flooding your scene with unnaturally perfect light of the same color and intensity all around. No wonder why it's so difficult to light in 3D!

Natural Lighting introduces 10 31 (and counting!) beautiful animated lighting setups straight from the asset browser that will surely make your scenes reach another level of realism.


Simply drag & drop your preferred Natural Light into your scene and see the magic happen with fully animated effects controllable right from the 3D viewport interface.

Natural Lighting works great with Cycles and EEVEE volumetrics producing gorgeous animated god-rays.


It's so easy to use

Natural Lighting is designed to be as easy to use as possible. Simply drag & drop your Natural Light into your scene. Each light will create it's own custom user interface in the side panel!
