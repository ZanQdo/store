A Powerful Toolkit for Every Rigger

Whether you're building a custom rig from scratch or animating a complex character, these tools are designed to speed up your work and simplify common tasks.

Accelerated Weight Painting

Jump into a perfectly configured weight-painting environment with a single click. Automatically enable X-Ray, show deform bones, and isolate your character for a focused, frustration-free skinning session. Transfer, merge, and clean weights with powerful, intuitive operators.

Smart Workflow Utilities

A collection of intelligent tools to solve daily rigging challenges:

  • Instant Custom Shape Editing: Select a bone and instantly enter Edit Mode on its custom shape. No more searching through collections!

  • Global Modifier Toggle: Instantly boost viewport performance by toggling heavy modifiers (like Subdivision) across your entire character with one click.

  • Batch Operations: Change rotation modes, reset constraint distances, or set B-Bone scaling for the whole rig at once.

Effortless Scene Cleanup

Sanitize your models with one-click buttons to remove unused vertex groups, old UV maps, custom normals, and other messy data that bloats your files and causes export errors.

Revolutionary Workflow for Rigify Users

This is a game-changer. The old workflow forced you to lose any custom edits made to your generated rig. Now, you can freely add constraints, drivers, and custom shapes directly to the generated rig, then go back and update your metarig. When you regenerate, Pataz Rigging Toolz intelligently reapplies those custom edits for you. It’s a truly non-destructive pipeline.

  • ✅ Preserve Bone Constraints: Add, edit, or even reorder constraints on your final, generated rig. Your entire constraint stack will be perfectly rebuilt after you regenerate.

  • ✅ Preserve Custom Shapes: Assign custom shapes and tweak their transforms on the generated rig. Those changes will stick, even after regeneration.

  • ✅ Preserve Custom Properties & Drivers: Add custom properties and complex drivers directly to the bones of your generated rig. They will be saved and reapplied automatically.

  • ✅ And so much more: Bendy Bones settings, bone layers, IK properties, and viewport display changes made to the generated rig are all preserved.
