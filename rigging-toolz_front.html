Revolutionary Workflow for Rigify Users

Stop the technical grind. Safely edit your final rig and regenerate without losing work.

The old workflow forced you to lose custom edits. Now, freely add constraints, drivers, and shapes directly to the generated rig, then go back and update your metarig. When you regenerate, Pataz Rigging Toolz intelligently reapplies them.

✅ Preserve Bone Constraints: Add, edit, or reorder constraints on your final rig; they rebuild perfectly.
✅ Preserve Custom Shapes: Assign custom shapes and tweak transforms on the generated rig.
✅ Preserve Properties & Drivers: Add custom properties and complex drivers directly to bones.
✅ And so much more: Bendy Bones settings, bone layers, IK properties, and viewport display changes are preserved.
