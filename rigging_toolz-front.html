Smart Workflow Utilities

Intelligent tools to solve daily rigging challenges:

Instant Custom Shape Editing: Select a bone and instantly enter Edit Mode on its custom shape.
Global Modifier Toggle: Instantly boost viewport performance by toggling heavy modifiers (like Subdivision) across your entire character.
Batch Operations: Change rotation modes, reset constraint distances, or set B-Bone scaling for the whole rig at once.

Effortless Scene Cleanup

Sanitize your models with one-click buttons. Remove unused vertex groups, old UV maps, custom normals, and other messy data that bloats files and causes export errors.

Rigify Revolution

Safely edit your final rig and regenerate without losing work.

The old workflow forced you to lose custom edits. Now, freely add constraints, drivers, and shapes directly to the generated rig, then go back and update your metarig. When you regenerate, Pataz Rigging Toolz intelligently reapplies them.
